SEC. 01 // DRILL LOG

HOW DETECTION WORKS

01

Flakiness isn't a bug you find — it's a signal you record. Four stages, zero rewrites.

01

Attach the sensors

One GitHub Action, zero rewrites. Pritahi/falsky-action@v1 hooks into your runner and CI in under two minutes. Every run, every retry, every timing wobble gets timestamped and sent to Falsky.

02

Record every tremor

Passes that needed retries, timing jitter, order dependence, fixture bleed — each one leaves a waveform. "Green by luck" stops being invisible the moment it's on the drum.

03

Locate the epicenter

Bayesian scoring and z-score anomaly detection triangulate root cause — shared state, leaked randomness, network ghosts, resource races — down to the category. Not a stack trace. A verdict.

04

Issue foreshock alerts

Quarantine a test before it shakes main. Alerts fire when a test's flakiness magnitude trends upward — not after the build is already red and the blame game has begun.
